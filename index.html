LOTOLOCO

Slingo meets Mexican Loteria

A slot-machine bingo hybrid that fuses classic Slingo spinning mechanics with a 75-card Mexican Loteria deck (90 for 6x6 boards), wrapped in a stylized Coco-inspired fiesta art direction. 150 levels across 5 themed worlds, server-authoritative architecture, ethical F2P monetisation.

Core Gameplay Loop
🎟

1. Spend a Ticket

Each level costs 1 ticket to enter. Tickets regenerate 1 every 2 hours both offline (capped at 5) and during active play (uncapped). This energy gate creates natural session breaks and return-visit motivation.

🎰

2. Spin the Reels

5 classic slot reels (6 for larger boards) spin and stop left-to-right with cascade timing. Each reel reveals a Loteria card or special symbol from that column's card range.

✅

3. Match & Daub

If a reel result matches a card on your board, it's automatically daubed. Each match scores 50 points. The server validates all matches — no client-side cheating.

🎉

4. Complete LOCOs

Complete a full row, column, or diagonal for a LOCO — worth 1,000 points each. Clear the entire board for a 5,000-point jackpot bonus.

⭐

5. Earn Stars & Coins

Your final score determines your star rating (1-3 stars). Stars unlock coin bonuses. Coins buy power-ups and extra spins for future levels.

🗺

6. Progress on the Map

Return to the World Map with your next level unlocked. Journey from El Mercado through La Playa, El Pueblo, La Fiesta, to the celestial El Cielo.

Special Reel Symbols
🃏

El Comodin — Column Wild

Player chooses any unmarked card in that column to daub. The strategic choice: complete a near-LOCO row, or set up for a future diagonal?

☀️

El Sol Dorado — Board Wild

The most powerful symbol. Player picks ANY unmarked cell on the entire board. Rare (1-1.5% per reel) but game-changing when it appears.

😈

El Diablito — Blocker

A dead result — no match possible for that reel. Introduced at Medium difficulty (level 16+). The frustration mechanic that makes wilds feel rewarding by contrast.

🎰

La Ruleta — Free Spin

Awards +1 extra spin, extending the level. A lifeline when you're close to a LOCO but running out of turns. Appears at 1.5-2% per reel.

Design Rule:

Total positive symbol chance (wilds + free spins) must ALWAYS exceed blocker chance. Even at Expert difficulty (4% blocker), positives are 6.5%. The game should always feel more generous than punishing.

Board Mechanics
📐

Progressive Board Size

Tutorial through Hard: 5x5 boards (25 cards, 75-card pool). Very Hard and Expert: 6x6 boards (36 cards, 90-card pool). Bigger boards = more cells to clear, more possible LOCOs, higher skill ceiling.

🔒

Locked Cells

Introduced at Medium (level 16). Locked cells need TWO matches to daub — first match cracks the lock, second marks it. Adds strategic depth: do you waste a wild on a locked cell, or target an easier LOCO?

✖️

Multiplier Cells

Introduced at Hard (level 31). Multiplier cells grant 2x points when matched (50 → 100 pts). LOCOs containing a multiplier cell score 2,000 instead of 1,000. Creates high-value targets on the board.

🏛️

Slingo Column Restrictions

Each column on the board only contains cards from a specific range (col 0 = cards 1-15, col 1 = 16-30, etc.). Each reel can only show cards from its column's range. This is the core Slingo mechanic that prevents any single spin from matching the whole board.

Engagement Systems
📅

Daily Login Rewards

7-day repeating cycle escalating from 1,000 coins to a Day 7 jackpot of 5,000 coins + 15 gems + 2 tickets. Missing a day doesn't reset the streak — it pauses it.

🐷

Piñata Bank

10% of all earned coins auto-save to the piñata bank. When full, pay gems to "break" it. 4 tiers (Ceramic → Gold) with increasing capacity. The break cost is always a better deal than buying coins directly.

🏆

Leaderboards

Weekly "La Semana" and monthly "El Mes" leaderboards tracking stars earned. Top players win coins, gems, tickets, and exclusive frames/titles.

🎬

Rewarded Ads

5 ad placements with daily caps: free tickets (3/day), double coins (5/day), free power-ups (2/day), coin bonus (10/day). All limits enforced server-side.

🏷️

Daily Deals

Rotating store deals with 20-50% discounts. Creates urgency (FOMO) and gives players a reason to check the store daily even when not planning to buy.

Technical Architecture
🛡️

Server-Authoritative

ALL game logic runs on PlayFab CloudScript — board generation, RNG, scoring, currency operations. The Unity client is a display layer. 35 CloudScript handlers, 4,700 lines of server code.

🔒

Anti-Cheat by Design

No client-side RNG, no local currency storage, no save-file manipulation. IAP receipt validation against App Store and Google Play. Ticket regen uses server timestamps — no clock exploits.

📱

Unity 2D (URP)

~40 C# scripts across 6 namespaces. Service locator pattern for dependency injection. Separate gameplay, UI, data, and PlayFab integration layers.

☁️

PlayFab Backend

Economy v2 for inventory, Player Data for state, Leaderboards v2 for rankings. Per-player sequential execution — no race conditions, no locking needed.

Board Generation Algorithm

Column-Restricted Card Assignment

From CloudScript generateBoard(size, poolSize):

1. Initialize an empty size x size grid
2. For each column c (0 to size-1):
   a. Get the card range for column c

Phase 1 — Retention Hooks
Collection System
Lotería Card Gallery Medium

Collection System — Lotería Card Gallery

Creates a parallel progression layer beyond level completion. Coin Master's card collection is their single most powerful retention mechanic.
Medium Effort High Retention Impact
  • Players collect 75 Lotería cards through gameplay — cards drop randomly on match
  • Rarity tiers: Common (30), Uncommon (12), Rare (8), Legendary (4)
  • Themed sets ("La Familia," "Los Animales," "La Fiesta," "El Cielo") — completing a set grants gems
  • Duplicates can be gifted to friends or recycled for coins
  • Gallery screen with silhouettes for undiscovered cards
Push Notifications
Strategic Return Hooks Low

Push Notification Strategy

Ticket regeneration and daily login streaks require the player to remember to come back. Notifications close that gap.
Low Effort High Retention Impact
  • "Your tickets are full!" — when 5 tickets available
  • "Your daily streak is at risk!" — 2 hours before daily reset
  • "Your piñata bank is almost full!" — at 80% capacity
  • "New daily deals!" — when deals rotate
  • "Your friends need help!" — social feature trigger
Phase 2 — Social Systems
Friend System
PlayFab Friends + Leaderboards Medium

Friend System

Social connections are the single biggest predictor of long-term retention in casual games. Candy Crush's life-gifting system is famously one of King's best retention hooks.
Medium Effort High Retention Impact
  • Add friends via PlayFab, Facebook, Apple Game Center
  • Friend list: avatar, level, last-active time
  • Friends highlighted on weekly/monthly leaderboards
  • Referral bonus: both players get 500 coins + 1 ticket
  • Max 50 friends (same as Coin Master)
Gifting
Daily Gift Reciprocity Loop Medium

Gifting System

Gifting creates reciprocity loops — "they sent me a ticket, I should send one back." Drives daily engagement even without personal motivation.
Medium Effort High Retention Impact
  • Send 1 free ticket OR 500 coins to a friend (daily limit per friend)
  • Gifts appear as popup on app launch
  • Gift is generated, not transferred — costs the sender nothing
  • Receive limit: 5 gifts per day (prevents alt-account exploitation)
Clubs (Familias)
20-Player Groups + Club Challenges High

Clubs — Familias

Club membership creates social obligation — "I can't let my familia down." The deepest retention hook in social games.
High Effort High Retention Impact
  • Create or join a "Familia" (up to 20 players)
  • Club leaderboard: combined weekly stars of all members
  • Club challenges: "Complete 100 levels as a club this week"
  • Club chat: pre-set messages only (avoids moderation costs)
  • Club rewards: bonus coins for all members when goals are met

Event System

Events create urgency (FOMO) and revenue spikes. Candy Crush's All-Stars tournament generated $108M in a single month.
High Effort High Revenue Impact
  • Weekend Tournament: 48 hours, separate leaderboard, themed rewards
  • Holiday Events: Día de los Muertos, Cinco de Mayo, Día de los Reyes, Las Posadas, Carnaval
  • Challenge Events: "Clear 10 boards in 24 hours" with milestone rewards
  • Special Board Events: All-wild reels, double Diablitos, giant 7×7 board

Cosmetics System
Card Backs, Themes, Markers, Frames Medium

Cosmetics System

Cosmetics are a proven non-pay-to-win monetisation layer. They give paying players visible status without gameplay advantage.
Medium Effort Medium Revenue Impact
  • Card Backs: Visible on social/leaderboard. Unlocked through collection, events, or purchase
  • Board Themes: Visual reskin (El Mercado, La Playa tropical, Día de Muertos marigold, etc.)
  • Daub Markers: Different match styles (classic circle, heart, star, skull, etc.)
  • Profile Frames: Border around avatar on leaderboards. Earned from ranking high
  • Name Effects: Animated name (gold, fire, rainbow, etc.) — 10 effects with gem pricing
Phase 4 — Endgame
Endless Mode
Procedural Boards + Escalating Difficulty Medium

Endless Mode

Players who complete all content churn immediately. Endless mode gives completionists infinite replayability.
Medium Effort Medium Retention Impact
  • Unlocks after level 150 (or as separate mode at level 50)
  • Board difficulty increases every 5 rounds (more locked cells, more blockers, larger boards)
  • No ticket cost (or reduced) — it's retention, not monetisation
  • Personal best leaderboard
  • Milestone rewards every 10 rounds (coins, gems, exclusive cosmetics)
Hard Mode Replays
Modified Rules + Bonus Rewards Low

Hard Mode Replays

Doubles content without creating new levels. Gives star-hunters a reason to replay.
Low Effort Low Retention Impact
  • "Hard Mode" toggle on completed levels
  • Modifications: fewer spins (−2), more blockers (+2%), more locked cells (+2)
  • Rewards: bonus stars, exclusive "hard mode" badge, extra coins
  • Hard mode stars tracked separately (don't overwrite normal stars)
